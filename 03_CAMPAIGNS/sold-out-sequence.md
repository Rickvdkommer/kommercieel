## Day 4: THE PLAN — "How to Build a GTM Engine in 8 Weeks (The Honest Version)"

**Format:** LinkedIn carousel or long-form post

---

I'll give you the full playbook. No gatekeeping.

**Week 1–2: Infrastructure**
- Buy 3–5 sending domains ($12/year each). Set up SPF, DKIM, DMARC.
- Warm them for 14 days (use Instantly or Lemlist warmup).
- Set up your CRM pipeline stages. No more than 6 stages.
- Connect Slack alerts for new leads entering pipeline.

**Week 3–4: Data**
- Define your ICP in writing. Company size, industry, title, geo, qualifying signals.
- Use Bitscale or Clay to build enriched lead lists. Minimum: verified email, LinkedIn URL, company data.
- Score leads: A (perfect fit), B (probable fit), C (worth testing). Only sequence A and B.

**Week 5–6: Sequences**
- Write 3 cold email sequences (different angles: pain, proof, direct).
- Write LinkedIn connection request + 3-message follow-up.
- Set up multi-channel: email day 1, LinkedIn day 3, email day 5, email day 8, breakup day 12.
- A/B test subject lines from day one.

**Week 7–8: Automation + Optimization**
- Build lead qualification rules. Auto-tag by engagement.
- Set up pipeline automation: move stages on reply, alert on positive sentiment.
- Review first 2 weeks of data. Pause underperformers. Double down on winners.
- Document everything in a runbook.

That's the blueprint. It works. We've built it.

Here's what I won't pretend: executing this while also running your company, building product, managing a team, and fundraising is realistic. The steps are simple. Doing them well, in sequence, while maintaining quality on the data and copy — that's the actual job.
