# Kommercieel Target ICP

## Primary Target
- **Stage:** Seed / Series A B2B SaaS companies ($0–5M ARR)
- **Problem:** No GTM team or infrastructure yet, founders doing sales manually
- **Need:** Outbound engine, lead gen systems, pipeline automation
- **Budget range:** $1,500–2,500/month retainer

## Why This ICP
- Can't afford full-time GTM hire ($80K+/year) but need the systems
- Founders are technical, appreciate automation-first approach
- Quick decision-makers (no procurement process)
- Willing to trade testimonial/case study for discounted rate ("First Five")

## Qualifying Signals
- Recently raised seed/Series A
- Hiring for first sales/GTM roles (but haven't filled them)
- Founder still doing outbound manually
- Active on LinkedIn talking about GTM challenges
- Using basic tools (spreadsheets, manual email) instead of proper stack

## Where to Find Them
- LinkedIn (founder posts about GTM struggles)
- Crunchbase / PitchBook (recent funding rounds)
- YC / accelerator demo days
- Bitscale enrichment from competitor audiences
- Referrals from GTM Legends network
